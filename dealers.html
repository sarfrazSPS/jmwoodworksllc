Dealers

J&M Woodworks is happy to offer our product line to new dealers depending on location. We are careful to protect the geographic area of our dealers. As an Amish manufacturer of the well known Eagle Collection that consist of Amish built sheds, pool houses, and custom garages, we ensure that all our products are the highest possible quality that offers well above industry workmanship and materials.

As a dealer of J&M Woodworks, you will also have access to many tools needed to assist in building your business. Our back room offers images of all our products, wholesale price list, catalogs, and marketing material.

We are also happy to offer complete training on the quality of our outdoor structures.

If you would like to become a dealer of J&M Woodworks, simply click below to complete an application. We will get back to you as soon as possible.
