About JM Woodworks

J&M Woodworks is an Amish wholesale shed manufacturer in Lancaster PA. We pride ourselves in knowing we create quality products that will last. For over 30 years, we have created the finest products including custom sheds, garages, and even pool houses.

J&M Woodworks is happy to offer many options and quality features that are far above the industry standard. You are able to find the perfect shed, choose the colors for the siding, trim, roof, windows, doors,and so much more. There are many options to choose from when creating a shed, pool house, or garage.
